# Code of Conduct

We're committed to building a welcoming, inclusive community where everyone feels safe contributing, asking questions, and sharing ideas.

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, sexual identity and orientation, or socioeconomic status.

## Our Standards

**Positive examples of behavior include:**
- Using welcoming, inclusive language
- Being respectful of different viewpoints and experiences
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully
- Focusing on what's best for the community
- Showing empathy toward other community members

**Unacceptable behavior includes:**
- Harassment, discrimination, or derogatory comments
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory remarks, or personal/political attacks
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Any form of unwelcome sexual attention or behavior
- Any other conduct that reasonably could be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our community standards. We will take appropriate and fair action in response to any behavior that violates this Code of Conduct.

**Report violations:**
- If safe, [open a GitHub Issue](https://github.com/composable-delivery/snowfakery-mcp/issues)
- For serious concerns, contact maintainers via [GitHub Security Advisories](https://github.com/composable-delivery/snowfakery-mcp/security/advisories) or reach out privately

Maintainers are committed to:
- Investigating reports promptly and fairly
- Protecting the privacy of the reporter
- Taking appropriate remedial action

We're building this community for everyone. Thank you for helping us maintain a respectful, productive space.
